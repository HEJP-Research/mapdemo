HEJP Post-doc Preliminary Tool

Below are sample images from the various modes of this tool that allows for data analysis of Higher Education Postdoctoral Job postings. This includes 3 images of the map mode, 3 images of the bar graph mode, and 3 images of the line graph mode. If you hover over a mode, you will see 1 of the sample images for the mode. Click on a mode to be redirected to that mode. From within each mode, you can navigate to the other modes. If you wish to continue previewing the other modes after hovering over a mode, click "Keep Previewing".
